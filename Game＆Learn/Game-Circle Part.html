Theorem 1
Angle at the Centre

The angle at the centre is twice the angle at the circumference standing on the same arc.

Theorem 2
Angles in a Semicircle

The angle subtended by a diameter at the circumference is always a right angle.

Theorem 3
Angles in the Same Segment

Angles subtended by the same arc at the circumference are equal.

Theorem 4
Cyclic Quadrilateral

The opposite angles in a cyclic quadrilateral add up to 180 degrees.

Theorem 5
Radius to a Tangent

A radius is perpendicular to the tangent at the point of contact.

Theorem 6
Equal Tangents from One Point

Tangents drawn from the same external point to a circle have equal lengths.

Theorem 7
Angle Bisector of Two Tangents

The line from the external point to the centre bisects the angle between the two tangents.

Theorem 8
Alternate Segment Theorem

The angle between a tangent and a chord equals the angle in the alternate segment.
