How to play

Move: A / D or ← / →
Attack: Click the target you believe is correct
Lose HP when: You hit the wrong answer, an enemy escapes, or an enemy hits your plane
Wave rule: Each round shows 3 answer targets; clear the wave to move on
Unlock rule: Finish the run to unlock the next plane automatically
